Who we are

At Shanti, we believe that mental health is as important as physical health. The mind, body, and soul must be kept in balance for whole well-being. However, in today's world, most people neglect their mental health. This leads to the rise of mental health issues such as depression, anxiety, etc. Shanti will help you to regain your inner peace. Click below to get started!


How we help

In order to help your mental health, we have quite a few features. You can consult with one of our therapists, via chat or call. Click the tab "Therapist Chat" to get started. Please note that all of the communication will remain confidential.


Moreover, It has been shown in many studies that meditation helps with mental health issues, especially anxiety and depression. Click the link to get the meditation audio.

Additional Features

If you would like to help your fellow human beings, you can volunteer to be a therapist. You can also just listen to them, without providing advice. If interested, head over to the Volunteer tab to get sign up.
